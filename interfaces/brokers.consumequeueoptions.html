autoAck

autoAck: boolean
  • Defined in libs/brokers/src/brokers/BrokerUtil.ts:32

Wether or not messages should be automatically acknowledged WARNING: You are responsible for turning this off. Failing to handle your promises could lead to serious cases your code was probably not written to account, your AMQP server just doing its own thing. No matter what, make sure you use <a href="../classes/brokers.broker.html#channel">Broker.channel</a>.ack or <a href="../classes/brokers.broker.html#channel">Broker.channel</a>.reject once you're done processing something You should absolutely acknowledge messages you've successfully eaten and picking your own reject strategies for errors.
